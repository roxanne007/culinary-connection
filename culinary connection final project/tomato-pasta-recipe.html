Creamy Tomato Pasta is a delightful and comforting dish that combines the richness of a creamy tomato sauce with perfectly cooked pasta. This recipe is quick, easy, and bursting with flavor, making it a favorite for both weeknight dinners and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (about 2 cups) of your favorite pasta (such as spaghetti or fettuccine)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Step 2: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Step 3: Add the finely chopped onion and saut√© for about 2-3 minutes, or until translucent.
  4. Step 4: Add the minced garlic and saut√© for another 30 seconds, until fragrant.
  5. Step 5: Stir in the diced tomatoes (with their juices) and bring to a simmer. Cook for 5-7 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly.
  6. Step 6: Reduce the heat to low and add the heavy cream, grated Parmesan cheese, dried basil, and dried oregano. Stir until the sauce is well combined and creamy. Simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  7. Step 7: Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Step 8: Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat the pasta evenly with the creamy tomato sauce.
  9. Step 9: Remove from heat and garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
  10. Step 10: Serve hot and enjoy your creamy tomato pasta!
